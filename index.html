Mango Sticky Rice!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups glutinous (sweet) rice
  • 1 1/3 cups well-stirred canned unsweetened coconut milk
  • 1/3 cup plus 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds, toasted lightly
  • 1 large mango, peeled, pitted, and cut into thin slices (at least 24)

Instructions

  1. Make Rice
    • Wash rice
    • Soak in cold water overnight
    • Drain rice well in a sieve
  2. Steam Rice
    • Set sieve over large deep saucepan of simmering water
    • Cover with towel, place lid, steam for 30-40 min til tender
  3. Sauce
    • In small saucepan bring 1 cup coconut milk to a boil with 1/3 cup sugar and salt
    • Stirring until sugar is dissolved
    • Remove from heat, keep warm
  4. Mix
    • Transfer cooked rice to a bowl and stir in coconut-milk mixture
    • Let rice stand, covered, 30 minutes (til mixture absorbed)
    • Remove from heat, keep warm
  5. Mango
    • Slice Mango into small bits
  6. Additional
    • Slowly boil 1/3 cup coconut milk with 3 tbsp sugar, stir occasionally
    • Transfer to bowl, let chill
  7. Plate + serve!
